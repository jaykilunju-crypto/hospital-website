Our Medical Services

At St. Josephine Mara Hospital we offer affordable health services to humanity — a 24-hour Level 4 hospital serving Naivasha and beyond.

🏥

In & Outpatient

Fully equipped outpatient with a competent team of doctors and nurses responding to emergencies, consultations and general care.

🤰

Maternity

Fully equipped maternity unit with modern equipment, specialist gynaecologist, paediatrician and 24-hour skilled delivery care.

🔪

Major & Minor Surgeries

Fully equipped operating theatre with modern machinery for emergency and general surgeries, including specialised procedures.

📡

Ultrasound & X-ray

Modern imaging machines for ultrasound, ECG, ECHO, X-ray and Doppler ultrasound operated by experienced radiologists.

🔬

Laboratory Services

24-hour fully equipped laboratory with state-of-the-art equipment and competent personnel to give accurate results at affordable prices.

Endoscopy & ECG

Antenatal & Postnatal

Our Story

A Legacy of Healing in the Heart of Kenya

Founded in 2023 by the Josephine Mara Foundation, our hospital began as a small clinic serving Naivasha's underserved communities. Today we stand as a 15+ -bed regional centre, equipped with technologies that rival the finest hospitals in East Africa — yet grounded in the same spirit of compassionate service.
